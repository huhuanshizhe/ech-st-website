About ECH-ST Electrics

Professional electrical manufacturing with over 20 years of experience

Leading Electrical Manufacturer in China

ECH-ST Electrics is a professional manufacturer specializing in circuit breakers, protection devices, and energy storage systems. With R&D centers and manufacturing facilities in Wenzhou and a supply chain center in Nanjing, we serve customers in over 50 countries worldwide.

Our product portfolio includes MCCB, MCB, SPD, MOV, Energy Storage Systems, IoT Smart Devices, and RS485 chips. We are committed to providing high-quality electrical solutions for residential, commercial, and industrial applications.
